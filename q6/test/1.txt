Trump, Cruz, Sanders each win 2 states

The results are in for the latest round of primaries and caucuses in the race for the US presidency. Real estate mogul Donald Trump and Senators Ted Cruz and Bernie Sanders have each won 2 states. Former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton came out ahead in one state.

Republicans held a primary in one state and caucuses in 3 others on Saturday. Trump won Louisiana and Kentucky. Cruz won Kansas and Maine.

With the latest results, Trump has now secured 12 states, while Cruz has won 6 states.

Democrats held a primary in one state and caucuses in 2 others on the same day. Sanders won Kansas and Nebraska. A major theme of his campaign is the growing gap between the rich and poor.

Clinton won the Louisiana Democratic primary. She has now secured 11 states, compared to 7 for Sanders.
